The three capabilities

The real challenge with process excellence

Turning a human-led process and its nuance into AI-ready documentation.

AI performs brilliantly on what is documented well. It won't carry the human nuance if that nuance was never captured. Bridging that gap needs three capabilities working together.

1 Understanding how the frontline works, and how their judgement can reach the SOP

Find the human judgement and nuance, and a way to capture it.

Someone who knows insurance processes well enough to sit with underwriters and claims people, talk through the technical detail of how a process actually works and how their thinking works, spot the nuance and judgement, and find ways to capture it into the documentation.

2 Capturing the nuance into the SOP

Structure the documentation so it includes the decisions under ambiguity, not a set of rigid steps.

The second step: taking those insights and putting them into a properly structured document that includes the decisions under ambiguity, not just a set of rigid steps. It is also about how a process and its Level 4 and 5 documentation affects the wider efficiency of the department.

3 Using the SOP for effective AI integration

Know where AI runs and where a human evaluates.

Understanding where AI can be used to improve efficiency, and where the human stays in the loop because customer outcomes are the ultimate value.

People and capability

The people who power it

A shift of focus for people on the frontline

The people on the frontline hold the deepest process knowledge.

As AI takes the manual load as an assistant, the people who hold the deepest process knowledge are freed up. The opportunity is to redeploy them into process excellence, identifying which micro-processes need documenting and feeding clean, structured, nuance-rich detail into the SOPs.

Resourcing process excellence

Resourcing AI-enabled improvement means resourcing these people.

Building AI is not mandatory for them. Their work here is giving thorough, high-quality documentation that carries the nuance of how they work, the detail that feeds effective AI integration.
